# flores_project_m1_report_2026
In this repository can be found all annexes of my M1 internship report for the Flores project (including figures, tables, etc.)

Annex A1 : informations on RENECOFOR sites

Annex A2 : information on
- estimation of
  - tree height : without considering competition, considering global competition (competition with all other trees on the stand) and considering relative competition (competition with trees that have a larger DBH only)
  - aboveground volume
  - crown cover
  - wood biomass
- allometric relations between wood and leaf biomass and between wood and fruit biomass on HET sites

Annex A3 : information on 
- comparison plot of estimated and predicted wood and leaf biomass, for each canopy layer (and for each species group for the wood biomass)
- linear models summary tables : summary tables of estimated and predicted biomass of wood, leaf and fruit biomass for each canopy layer and species group
- linear models summary tables on means : summary tables of the mean (for each site) estimated and predicted biomass of wood, leaf and fruit biomass for each canopy layer and species group
- plots of estimated and predicted biomass of wood, leaf and fruit biomass for each canopy layer and species group (the difference with the figures 4., 5. and 6. of the report is that each point corresponds to the biomass for one site and one year (in the report we calculated the mean biomass over all years for each site)
